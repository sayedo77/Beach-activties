Boats

Our beach boat trips are a great way to spend beautiful times with your loved one; our trips depart from 8 am to 4 pm, and this trip allows our customers to experience fishing and then go to Jerada Island to enjoy its nature and eat food. This island in Bahrain is characterized by its soft soil and the purity of its water. The cost of the private trip is 200 BD, and the cost of the general trip is BD 50

  • Private Trip: 200BD
  • Public Trip: 50BD

Jet Skis

Rent a jet ski from us to enjoy the beautiful waters of Bahrain and to explore Bahrain's marine sites. The rental service is open from 10 am to 6 pm, and the cost of renting a vehicle per hour is 60 BD

  • Price Per Person For 1 hour: 60BD
  • Group Price Over 3 People Per Hour: 30BD

Padel Game

On our beaches, there are distinct courts for the game of Padel with a beautiful sea view, and we offer our services for renting the court by the hour or for tournaments; we are open from 10 am to 10 pm.the price of booking the Padel court for an hour costs 30 BD.

  • Price Per Hour: 30BD
  • Tournaments: Contact Us Please

Diving Trip

Explore with us a diving trip in the seas of Bahrain, and you will be able to see the coral reefs, the wrecks of the ships and the plane we have, and see the fish as well, in addition to that, you can try oyster fishing to search for pearls. Our trip starts from 12 pm until 5 pm, and the cost of the trip is 130 BD.

  • Price Per Person: 130BD
  • Group Price Over 3 People : 100BD

Restaurant
